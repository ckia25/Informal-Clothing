  • General

  • What is inFormal?

    inFormal is a peer-to-peer clothing lending platform for Princeton students.

  • What can I do with inFormal?

    You can lend out your clothing, and borrowing clothing from other users!

  • Why was inFormal created?

    We wanted to give Princeton students a way to expand their wardrobe, and reduce their carbon footprint!

  • Who created inFormal?

    Brandon Cheng, Caiden Kiani, Jacob Santelli, Katie Heller, and Matt Drapkin!

  • What's next for inFormal?

    We are hoping to apply to become a TigerApp, so stay tuned!

  • Our Point System

  • What are points?

    Points are inFormal's currency!

  • What can I use points for?

    To rent clothes for some number of points/day!

  • How many points do users start with?

    500!

  • How do I get more points?

    By renting out your clothes! Click here to get started.

  • How do I check my balance

    In your profile! Click here to take a look.

  • How do I price my item?

    Here is what we suggest:

    Type Price (pts/day)
    Dresses 80-100
    Shoes 70-90
    Jacket 60-80
    Sweater 60-80
    Pants 50-70
    Bottoms 50-70
    Tops 40-60
    Sweatshirt 40-60
    Shirt 30-50
    Shorts 30-50

  • Borrowing

  • How do I borrow clothing?

    Go to the listings page to browse items!

  • How do borrowing charges work?

    When you request an item worth x pts/day for y days, your account is charged x*y points.

    If the lender approves your request, the exchange begins.

    If not, the points get refunded!

  • Lending

  • What happens if an item is overdue?

    Please reach out to the student! All users of inFormal are members of the Princeton community, and thus subject to the guidelines in Rights, Rules, and Responsibilities!

  • Privacy

  • If I add my phone number to my profile, is it public?

    No. Only users who you request to borrow from and users whose requests you approve can see your phone number.

  • Are all ratings public?

    Yes. All ratings and ratings messages are public.

  • Are my bookmarks public?

    Yes, the items you bookmark are visible to other users via your profile page.
